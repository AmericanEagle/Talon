Bring UCSD to Your WorkplaceJoin San Diego’s most innovative organizations in receiving customized on-site training from UC San Diego Extension. On-site education is the quickest, most reliable way to infuse your organization with concise, up-to-date information on timely trends and best practices.

The results are more advanced skills, better problem solving, and revitalized productivity. Employees also express greater job satisfaction by gaining valuable work knowledge while earning continuing education credits.

Benefits of Custom Training

UC Approved:

Coursework reflects the high academic standards of UCSD — a leader in continuing education.

Flexible & Convenient:

Courses are delivered to your employees, at your location, on your schedule. Some may even be delivered online.

Customized Content:

Each class is designed to match the size of your staff, the stage of your organization’s growth, and your particular industry.

Effortless:

Our goal is to make custom training easy for you. By listening to your needs and relying on our attentive staff, we can respond to you quickly and easily.

Applicable Knowledge:

Learn the skills and information that drive today’s business and technology trends. Better lead people, processes and projects.

Top Instructors:

Choose from a large cadre of faculty and industry experts who bring real-world knowledge to the table.

Cost Effective:

Custom training is competitively priced to fit your budget, eliminates travel time, and can directly impact the productivity of the organization.
